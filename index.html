Delicious Pasta Recipe

This pasta recipe is inspired by traditional Italian cuisine, known for its simplicity and rich flavors. It's perfect for a quick weeknight meal or a weekend dinner party with friends. The recipe has been passed down through generations and has become a family favorite.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity Substitute
Pasta (spaghetti) 200g Any type of pasta
Olive oil 2 tbsp Butter
Garlic 3 cloves Shallots
Parmesan cheese 50g Pecorino cheese
Fresh basil Handful Parsley

Instructions

  1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook the pasta until al dente. Tip: Don’t overcook the pasta; it should be slightly firm.
  2. In a pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat and sauté the garlic until golden. Tip: Be careful not to burn the garlic as it turns bitter.
  3. Drain the pasta and toss it into the pan with the garlic and olive oil. Mix well.
  4. Grate the Parmesan cheese over the pasta and mix until well combined.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and serve immediately.

Serving Tips

Serve this pasta dish with a light salad and a glass of white wine for the perfect meal. Tip: Warm the plates before serving to keep the pasta hot longer.
